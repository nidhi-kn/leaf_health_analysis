Technology Stack

Model Architecture: MobileNetV2 (CNN-based)

Training Method: Transfer Learning with ImageNet pre-training

Framework: TensorFlow/Keras

Web Framework: Flask

Dataset: PlantVillage Tomato Disease Dataset

Detectable Diseases

The system can identify the following tomato conditions:

  • Bacterial Spot
  • Early Blight
  • Late Blight
  • Leaf Mold
  • Septoria Leaf Spot
  • Spider Mites
  • Target Spot
  • Yellow Leaf Curl Virus
  • Mosaic Virus
  • Healthy Leaves

Research Contributions

This project represents a comprehensive comparative study of CNN vs Transformer architectures for plant disease classification, demonstrating:

  • Production-ready CNN performance (86.12% accuracy)
  • Successful Transformer training on small datasets (46.96% accuracy)
  • Ensemble methodology achieving 97.35% top-3 accuracy
  • Real-world applicability for agricultural AI
